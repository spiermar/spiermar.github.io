Rickshaw Run

In 2017, I participated in the Rickshaw Run, driving a tuk tuk in India, top to bottom, from Rajasthan to Kerala. Over 3.000 km, with a max speed of 30 km/h, going downhill.

Llama Run

Earlier in 2018, me and a few frieds decided to drive old VW Bugs from South Brazil to Peru! We lost a car (and a participant) on the way, but we managed to visit Iguazu Falls, cross the Andes, the Atacama desert, drove into Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia, went to Lake Titicaca and Machu Picchu, and arrived in Cusco to complete our month-long adventure.
