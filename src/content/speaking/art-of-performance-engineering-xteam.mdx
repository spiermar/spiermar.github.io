## Abstract

Resilient engineering teams are forged through trust, technical depth, and a culture that adapts to constant challenges. This episode explores the approach to fostering high-performance engineering culture, empowering teams through ownership, and prioritizing computer science fundamentals. Insights from scaling Netflix's cloud infrastructure to leading innovation at one of Latin America's largest digital wallets.
