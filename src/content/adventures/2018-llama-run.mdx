# Llama Run

Earlier in 2018, me and a few friends decided to drive old VW Bugs from South Brazil to Peru! We lost a car (and a participant) on the way, but we managed to visit incredible places along the route.

## The Route

Starting in Southern Brazil, we drove northwest through some of South America's most stunning landscapes:

- **Iguazu Falls**: One of the world's most spectacular waterfall systems
- **The Andes**: Crossing the mountain range at altitude
- **Atacama Desert**: The driest desert on Earth
- **Salar de Uyuni**: The world's largest salt flat in Bolivia
- **Lake Titicaca**: The highest navigable lake in the world
- **Machu Picchu**: The legendary Incan citadel
- **Cusco**: Our final destination after a month on the road

## The Vehicles

Classic VW Bugs—air-cooled, rear-engine, and definitely not designed for this terrain. One didn't make it, which is all part of the adventure.

## The Adventure

Overlanding in vintage vehicles teaches you patience, problem-solving, and the art of going with the flow. Every breakdown becomes a story, every detour a discovery.

A month-long adventure that proved the journey matters more than the destination.
