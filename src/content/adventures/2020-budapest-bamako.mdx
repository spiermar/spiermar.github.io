# Budapest-Bamako

The Budapest-Bamako is the largest charity rally in the world, crossing Europe and Africa from Hungary to Mali. An incredible journey through diverse landscapes, cultures, and challenges.

## The Route

Starting in Budapest, Hungary, and heading south through:

- **Europe**: Hungary, Slovenia, Italy, France, Spain
- **Morocco**: Crossing the Strait of Gibraltar into Africa
- **Western Sahara**: Endless desert roads and ocean-side tracks
- **Mauritania**: Sand, more sand, and challenging terrain
- **Mali**: The final destination, Bamako

## The Challenge

Unlike traditional rallies, Budapest-Bamako focuses on adventure and charity rather than speed. Teams navigate through:

- Sahara desert crossings
- Challenging border crossings
- Unpredictable terrain and weather
- Mechanical challenges far from civilization

## The Purpose

The rally raises funds and delivers supplies to communities in West Africa. It's about using adventure as a force for good—bringing attention and resources to regions that need support.

## Why Do It?

Because the world is meant to be explored, not just visited. True adventure lies beyond comfort zones, in the dust, heat, and unknown roads of a transcontinental journey.
