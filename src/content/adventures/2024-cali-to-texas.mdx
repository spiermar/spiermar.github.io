# California to Texas Motorcycle Ride

A cross-country motorcycle journey from the Pacific coast of California to the heart of Texas. Two wheels, open roads, and the freedom of the American Southwest.

## The Route

Starting on the California coast and heading east through:

- **California**: Coastal highways and desert landscapes
- **Arizona**: Red rock country and high desert
- **New Mexico**: Vast skies and mountain passes
- **Texas**: The final destination, everything bigger

## The Experience

Motorcycle travel offers a unique perspective—you're not just passing through, you're part of the landscape. Every temperature change, every scent, every turn is immediate and visceral.

### Highlights

- **Coastal Roads**: Starting with Pacific Ocean views
- **Desert Crossings**: Long stretches of open highway
- **Mountain Passes**: Elevation changes and sweeping curves
- **Small Towns**: Authentic American road trip culture

## The Freedom

There's something special about a motorcycle trip. The packing is minimal, the pace is your own, and the road becomes a meditation. It's not about the destination—it's about the ride itself.

## Why Motorcycle?

Because some roads are meant to be experienced, not just traveled. The motorcycle strips away the barriers between you and the world around you.
