# Sample Mountain Hike

This was an incredible day hiking through the Test Mountains. The weather was perfect, with clear skies and moderate temperatures throughout the day.

## The Trail

The trail began at the base camp, winding through dense forest before opening up to stunning alpine meadows. The ascent was gradual but steady, gaining approximately 2,500 feet over 6 miles.

## Highlights

- **Summit Views**: 360-degree panoramic views of the surrounding peaks
- **Wildlife**: Spotted several marmots and mountain goats along the way
- **Flora**: Wildflowers were in full bloom, creating colorful displays across the meadows

## Technical Details

- **Distance**: 12 miles round trip
- **Elevation Gain**: 2,500 feet
- **Time**: 7 hours total
- **Difficulty**: Moderate

The descent followed the same route, allowing us to appreciate different perspectives of the landscape we'd climbed through earlier in the day.
