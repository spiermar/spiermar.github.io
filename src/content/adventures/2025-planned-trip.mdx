## Objective

The goal is to reach the 13,500-foot summit via the North Ridge route, a classic alpine climb that combines rock, ice, and mixed terrain.

## Preparation

The North Ridge is rated 5.7, AI3, requiring proficiency in:

- Rock climbing (multi-pitch trad)
- Ice climbing (moderate grade)
- Snow/glacier travel
- Alpine navigation
