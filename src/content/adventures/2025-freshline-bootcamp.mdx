# Freshline Bootcamp

An intensive 10-day adventure motorcycle bootcamp through one of the harshest regions on Earth. Where limits are tested and spirit is ignited.

## The Challenge

The Freshline Bootcamp pushes riders both mentally and physically, preparing participants to ride anywhere on the planet. Namibia's extreme terrain provides the perfect classroom for developing expedition-ready skills.

## Curriculum

### Field Mechanics

Master essential bike repair and maintenance skills for expeditions:

- Daily pre-ride checks
- Troubleshooting fuel, electrical, and air systems
- Tire changes and field repairs with minimal tools

### Advanced ADV Riding

Push riding abilities through:

- Sand, mud, and rocky terrain navigation
- Challenging off-road techniques
- Bike recovery methods and towing

### Field Medical

Hands-on medical training for expedition scenarios:

- Emergency response and wound care
- Treating fractures with minimal resources
- Stabilizing riders in the field

### Expedition Prep & Logistics

- Route planning with GPS tools
- Medical and emergency plans
- Gear packing and logistics

### Expedition Team Leadership

- Team dynamics and communication
- Decision-making under pressure
- Leadership principles for mission success

## The Environment

Namibia offers some of the most challenging and beautiful terrain on Earth—endless desert landscapes, remote wilderness, and conditions that demand respect and preparation.
