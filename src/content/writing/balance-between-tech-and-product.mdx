- some problems need more tech, some more product
- each area will focus on what benefits them the most
- product will try to sell and deliver features
- tech will try to build something that doesn’t give them headaches in the future
- that balance is important to deliver a product in the right timeframe, and with the right quality
- at picpay, at the highest level, that balance does not exist, since tech “reports” to product and just executes
- so the tech agenda needs to be pushed by someone else, in this case, tech core
- that’s why we get into so many arguments with BUs
- we’re the ones at the frontlines of that discussion, to guarantee balance
