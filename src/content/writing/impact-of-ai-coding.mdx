- small companies with have a bigger advantage
- smaller companies, developers spend most of the time coding
- ai tools will help with coding activities only
- at larger corporations, most of time time spent by developers is in other tasks
- understanding requirements, understanding current systems, development process toil
- the larger the fraction of time spent on coding, the bigger improvement of AI tools
- AB test shown an 18% improvement in total functionality output
- Takes time for developers to get used to the new way of working
- Jr engineers who taken more advantage, since they are coding the most
- Sr engineers are focused on other parts of the process
- System Design, code reviews, etc
- It might eliminate Jr engineers in the future, since basic coding activities will be much faster, and could be taken by Sr engineers on the time they were spending helping Jr engineers
- Increase the need for Sr engineers
- Increase responsibility of engineering leaders
- More can be done with less engineers, so right direction is important
