draft: true
---

- If you prioritize what is urgent over what is important, you will always be reacting to change, instead of creating change.
- Urgent things tend to be reactive, fighting fires, repetitive tasks. Large changes generally don't have a due date, and take more effort.
- Prioritizing small urgent things will cause you not to make improvements, and be always chasing your tail.
