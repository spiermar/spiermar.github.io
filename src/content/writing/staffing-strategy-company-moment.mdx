- different maturity levels require different staffing strategies
- companies with little maturity, that are in the early stages of defining things, need more senior engineers
- to solve the problems and define how things will be in the future
- junior engineers are great at executing taks
- but when there is no structure telling junior engineers what and how to do things, they get lost and frustrated
- strategy needs to follow the company
- whant to hire junior engineers, you need the structure for them to learn and be productive
