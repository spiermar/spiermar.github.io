## The Story

Época Negócios, one of Brazil's leading business magazines, featured my career journey from a small town in southern Brazil to working at Netflix in Silicon Valley. The article highlighted how I became a Senior Performance Architect at Netflix, helping ensure millions of subscribers worldwide could stream their favorite shows without technical issues.

## What They Covered

- My path from Nova Petrópolis to Silicon Valley
- Building a performance engineering practice at Netflix
- The unique challenges of optimizing systems at massive scale
- What it means to work on problems no one has solved before
