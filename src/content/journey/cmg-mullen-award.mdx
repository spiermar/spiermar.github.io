## The Award

The Mullen Foundation was established in 1990 to honor the memory of J. William (Bill) Mullen, past president of CMG. The award recognizes individuals who demonstrate technical excellence combined with the ability to communicate effectively with each member of a wide audience.

## The Presentation

"Real User Performance Monitoring at Netflix Scale" covered how Netflix usefully aggregates enormous quantities of high-dimensional data to understand and improve user performance for over 130 million users watching more than 140 million hours of content daily.

## About CMG

CMG (Computer Measurement Group) is a highly influential worldwide organization of IT professionals committed to digital transformation initiatives and best practices. Founded in 1974, it has built a highly skilled membership network over the past 40 years.
